Frequently Asked Questions
Who can request a collab?
Anyone — developers, artists, musicians, writers, designers. If you have a creative idea and want a partner, apply.
Is InColab paid?
Depends on the project and scope. We'll discuss terms directly after reviewing your request — no assumptions either way.
How long does a response take?
We aim to respond within a few days. If we haven't replied in a week, feel free to follow up via itch.io messaging.
Can I collab with both?
Yes — send a request to either partner first. If both of us need to be involved we'll loop each other in during the discussion.
