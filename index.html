THE FOREST WHISPER

formerly: "The Lost Kid"

You play as a lost child wandering deep inside a dark, endless forest. There is no path, no map, no one to guide you. The deeper you go, the more the forest feels… wrong.

Strange creatures lurk between the trees — watching, waiting. Some chase you. Some simply exist in ways that don't make sense. Survive, explore, and uncover the truth hidden within.
