The Lost Kid (Now 'The Forest Whisper')

You play as a lost child wandering deep inside a dark, endless forest. There is no clear path, no map, and no one to guide you. The deeper you go, the more the forest begins to feel… wrong. Strange creatures lurk between the trees, watching, waiting. Some will chase you. Some will simply exist in ways that don’t make sense. Your goal is simple: survive, explore, and uncover the truth hidden within the forest.
